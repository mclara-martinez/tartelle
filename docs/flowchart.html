Key Pain Points
🔥
Overselling
Same product sold on WhatsApp AND Rappi before anyone realizes it's out of stock.
⏳
Manual Invoicing
50+ invoices/week created one-by-one in Siigo. Admin spends hours on data entry.
👻
Inventory Blindness
Siigo doesn't auto-decrement. Kitchen reports stock via WhatsApp at end of day.
📍
No Delivery Tracking
Customers don't know delivery status. Admin manually WhatsApps each update.
